About The Firm

Frank P. Key & Associates Architects is a full service firm dedicated to the design and execution of architectural projects which are responsible to client needs and improve community through the built environment. The firm was founded in 1991 and has been located in McAllen Texas exclusively for the past 15 years. The firm's philosophy provides for a continuous design process from concept through occupancy, since the design process never stops the final product meets or exceeds the conceptual intent. To assist the architectural process the firm uses the state of the art BIM design software always working in 3 dimensions through the entire project, this true virtual model, allows instant 3d views form any point facilitating input from architect, owner, and consultants. This model can be easily converted in a computer walk-thru for more thorough site contextual presentation.
